Let $AECF$ be a complete quadrilateral, and $AC$, $BD$, $EF$ his
diagonals. Let $P$ be the middle of $AC$, $Q$ the middle of $BD$,
and $R$ the middle of $EF$. Then $P, Q, R$ are on a same line,
called the \emph{Newton-Gauss line.}
