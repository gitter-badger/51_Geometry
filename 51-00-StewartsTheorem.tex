Let be given a triangle $\triangle ABC$ with $AB=c$, $BC=a$, $CA=b$, and a point $X$ on $BC$ such that $BX=m$ and $XC=n$. Denote with $p$ the length of $AX$.
\figura{stewart}
Then $$a(p^2+mn)=b^2m+c^2n.$$
