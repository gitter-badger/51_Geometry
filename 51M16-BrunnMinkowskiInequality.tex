Let $A$ and $B$ be non-empty compact subsets of $\mathbb{R}^d$.
Then
\begin{equation*}
\vol(A+B)^{1/d}\geq\vol(A)^{1/d}+\vol(B)^{1/d},
\end{equation*}
where $A+B$ denotes the Minkowski sum of $A$ and $B$, and $\vol(S)$ denotes the volume of $S$.
