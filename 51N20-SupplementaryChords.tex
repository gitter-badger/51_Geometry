Two chords of ellipse, connecting a point of the perimetre to the end-points of a \PMlinkname{diameter}{ConjugateDiametersOfEllipse}, are called {\em supplementary chords} of each other.

A pair of supplementary chords are parallel to a pair of conjugate diameters.
