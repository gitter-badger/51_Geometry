If a cyclic quadrilateral has sides $p,q,r,s$ then its area is given by
$$\sqrt{(T-p)(T-q)(T-r)(T-s)}$$
where $T=\frac{p+q+r+s}{2}$.

Note that if $s\to 0$, Heron's formula is recovered.
